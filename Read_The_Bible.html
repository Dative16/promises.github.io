Accept Jesus

Does Jesus matter?

No matter where you are in life, all of us struggle to find purpose, answers, and hope. Some days we just want to keep our heads above water – take care of our family, pay bills on time, and find some kind of work/life balance that keeps us healthy. But we also have deeper questions. Why am I here? What is the purpose of it all? Jesus matters because He came to answer all those deep questions we ask ourselves. He came to give us purpose in living our day-to-day lives and settles where we’ll spend eternity.

Jesus brings meaning to life.

Jesus brings meaning to life.

You don’t have to meet some impossible criteria or standard to know God – Jesus did that for you. God created you because he has a plan and purpose for your life. He is the only person capable of loving you unconditionally, knowing every need and hurt you have. We are all imperfect and without Jesus, we will not survive the pressures, expectations, and disappointments of this worldly standard. Jesus brings meaning and purpose to our lives so we can face each day knowing he will help us. Don’t believe the lie that you aren’t good enough, you need to clean up and change, or need to be perfect, to love Jesus. He already loves you.
