Accept Jesus

God wants to use you to make a difference!

No matter where you are in life, all of us struggle to find purpose, answers, and hope. Some days we just want to keep our heads above water – take care of our family, pay bills on time, and find some kind of work/life balance that keeps us healthy. But we also have deeper questions. Why am I here? What is the purpose of it all? Jesus matters because He came to answer all those deep questions we ask ourselves. He came to give us purpose in living our day-to-day lives and settles where we’ll spend eternity.

Whether you love building relationships, praying for others, or gathering people together, you can make an impact!

There are several opportunities to volunteer at All Gog's promises Ministries from wherever you are including in-person at a physical location, All Gog's promises Ministries Online & more!

Jesus brings meaning to life.

Jesus brings meaning to life.

Each week at All God's Promises Ministries, hundreds of people just like you give their time to serve. Whether it’s helping children learn about Jesus on their level at HFKidz, greeting first-time guests, or working behind the scenes, every volunteer is making a difference at All God's Promises Ministries! We know you’re busy, and a hundred things are competing for your time. But we also know you’ll grow in your relationship with Jesus and meet new friends when you start volunteering with a Host Team!

  • Why do we think All Gog's Promises Ministries is Home?

    "It’s where the broken can find healing, grace is given to those with messy lives, the lonely are met with hugs, hope to rebuild is shared with families torn apart and compassion is extended to the dark corners of the world where there is injustice. We are united, devoted, and together we look forward to seeing God move in our midst, believing the best is yet to come.
