Get connected

We are all created for community. God has designed us for relationships. Life is so much better when we are connected with others. A small group is a community committed to loving God and loving others. Getting involved in a small group offers several benefits such as: deepening your walk with Christ, celebrating life’s joys with others, encouraging each other to grow spiritually, developing a deeper understanding of the connection between God’s Word and our everyday life, and support when life gets difficult or tragedy hits.
